Chef & Owner

Sanjeev Kapoor is an Indian celebrity chef,
entrepreneur writer and television personality.
Kapoor stars in the TV show Khana Khazana,
which is the longest running show of its kind in Asia.
It broadcasts in 120 countries and
in 2010 had more than 500 million viewers.
He also launched his own Food Food channel,
in January 2011.
Discovery Communications through its Indian organization,
has acquired a majority stake in Kapoor's channel.
He was also a contestant on Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa.
